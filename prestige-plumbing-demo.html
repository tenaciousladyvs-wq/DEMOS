Who we are

Three decades of doing it the right way.

Prestige was founded in North Aurora in 1989. Through the boom years we built and serviced thousands of homes across the Fox Valley — and today we run lean and focused, specializing in the maintenance, repair, and replacement work that keeps your home running.

When you call Prestige, you reach people who have been doing this for a generation. We show up when we say we will, explain your options in plain language, and stand behind the work.

What that means for you

A smaller, owner-led team means the person who answers the phone is the person accountable for the job. You won't be passed around — and you won't be sold something you don't need.
